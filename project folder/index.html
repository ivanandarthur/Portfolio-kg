My Work

Squirrel Away

A savings app concept chosen specifically for its unfamiliarity to me. Never having used one, I thought it would be a great learning opportunity. After a few false starts, it came together due to excellent feedback from prototype testing. Pen and paper sketching is a weakness of mine but with this app I began to see improvement.

A Place Like Home

This concept was chosen after a conversation with friends who experienced the wrenching process of finding alternative housing for an older person and a developmentally challenged son. Usability tests and feedback were tremendously valuable, and more than a few users commented that they would love to see this become a full-fledged application.
